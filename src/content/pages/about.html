About Us

Meet Craig

Hey there - I'm Craig, the owner and operator of Root Design & Develop. I'm a web designer and developer based in Indianapolis, Indiana. I've been building websites for a couple of years - before that I owned and operated a couple of businesses with my wife. I'm passionate about helping small businesses and individuals build their online presence. I believe that a website is a powerful tool that can help you grow your business and reach your goals. I'm here to help you do that.

I have discovered that many small businesses and individuals are intimidated by the idea of building a website. I was one of them! We either decide to use a page builder to save money or are forced to spend thousands up front to have a site built. Those two options land at the far ends of a spectrum, and I'm hopeful that I can help bridge that gap. I'll work with you to build a website that you love and that will help you reach your goals. I'll take care of the technical stuff so you can focus on what you do best. I'll be here to answer any questions you have along the way. And I'll do that at a price that won't break the bank.

In my time away from building websites, I enjoy spending time with my wife and daughter, cooking, and being outside. I love that this job allows me to work with people from all over while still being able to spend time with my family.

I'd love the opportunity to work with you and to help tell the story of your business while we also attract new clients or customers! Please don't hesitate to reach out! Craig Sanders
